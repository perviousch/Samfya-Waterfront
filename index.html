Our Story

Nestled along the pristine shores of Lake Bangweulu, Samfya Waterfront Lodge offers an unparalleled experience of luxury and natural beauty. Our lodge combines modern comfort with traditional Zambian hospitality, creating a perfect retreat for both leisure and business travelers.

With stunning views of the lake, comfortable accommodations, and world-class amenities, we ensure every guest enjoys a memorable stay in the heart of Luapula Province.
